About

Hi, I'm Hemanth — a lifestyle and event photographer specializing in Pre-wedding, Baby Shower, Gender Reveal, Portraits and more.

Photography has always been my passion; I fell in love with the craft long before I owned my first camera — chasing light through windows, noticing tiny details others might miss. Over the years that curiosity became LCA – Lights Camera Action: a promise to tell stories that feel true to you. I started shooting friends and family events, learning how moments unfold when people forget about the camera.

Today I specialize in lifestyle, cultural and family celebrations — from Pre-Wedding warmth to Housewarming joy. My style blends candid emotion with clean, editorial frames so every gallery reads like a chapter from your life.
